General purpose transistor

 

A transistor can modify and/or amplify electrical power and electronic signals.

A transistor is made of a semiconductor material with a solid and non-moving part to pass a charge, and three or more pins that connect it to an external circuit.

In this video, you learn in more details what a transistor is, its working principles and some applications.
