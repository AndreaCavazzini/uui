Conductive thread

 A little thicker than every day thread, but still thin enough to be sewn in medium-eye needles or with a sewing machine that can handle "heavy" thread.

It is ideal for any wearable/e-textile project. It is made of stainless steel fibers, thus your projects will not "stop working" because of oxidation after a few months and its safe to wash.

In Conductive Thread - 10 tips you get inspiration for your project!
