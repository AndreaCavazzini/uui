Conductive paint

Paint that allows electricity to flow.

Circuits can be painted on different materials including paper, cardboard, wood, metal, etc. Need to wait for paint to dry.

This video will upgrade your ideas for your projects.

Next you can try to develop different types of projects, like this proximity sensor with electric paint as the video shows.
