Potentiometer

A manually adjustable, variable resistor with three terminals. Two terminals are connected to a resistive element, the third terminal is connected to an adjustable wiper.

The position of the wiper determines the output voltage.

Here you learn how to wire a pontentiometer.

In this video you learn how to use it and add to your circuit.
