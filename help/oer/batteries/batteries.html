Batteries

An electric battery is a device with electrochemical cells that transform a chemical reaction into electric current to power other devices.

Its positive terminal is called cathode and the negative is anode.

Batteries are useful if your project cannot be powered by the Arduino. All kind of batteries might be used (from a standard AAA, coin, lithium, etc.).
