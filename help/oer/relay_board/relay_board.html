Relay board

 
 

The relay board can be attached to your project to turn on/off lights, fans and other devices while keeping them isolated from your microcontroller.

In this tutorial, you can learn the basics about relays and how to add them to your projects.

Here you learn how to use 5V relay with arduino to turn ON/OFF a bulb.
