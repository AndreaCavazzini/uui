Arduino prototyping shield

The protoshield helps you to design your circuits and customize them according to your project goals.

You can connect SMD ICs ir TH to the prototyping area, you can stick a breadboard for test operations (as the figure shows) and the shield comes with different key features that will boost your plans, such as IOREF and GND power lines, LEDs and resistor footprints, among others.
