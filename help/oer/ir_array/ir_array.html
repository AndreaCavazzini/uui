IR array

It senses the presence or the absence of a line or an object moving across a line.

This device has IR (infrared) sensors that sends and receives LEDs. First, the transmitter LEDs emits light that the IR array receives back, when the light hits a reflective surface.

Here you find a in-depth manual about IR arrays.

In this tutorial you learn how to make a line follower robot.
